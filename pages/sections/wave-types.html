Types of Waves

There are also two main classes of waves, distinguished by the direction the medium oscillates in.

Transverse waves consist of oscillations perpendicular to the direction of the wave. Notice how the circles are oscillating in the vertical direction (up and down), perpendicular to the rightwards direction of the wave.

Longitudinal waves consist of oscillations parallel to the direction of the wave. There are moving areas of compression and rarefaction.
